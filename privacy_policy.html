Privacy Policy

Effective date: 08 July 2026 — Applies to the app "Receipt Maker: Rent & Invoice"
In short: Receipt Maker does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal or business data on any server. Everything you enter — landlord/tenant details, amounts, signatures, and receipt history — stays only on your own device.

1. Information We Collect

Receipt Maker is an offline receipt and invoice generator. The app does not require an internet connection, an account, or a login to function. We do not collect, receive, or have access to:

  • Names, addresses, PAN numbers, or other identity details you enter into receipts
  • Rent, sale, service, donation, or rental amounts you record
  • Signatures drawn inside the app
  • Your device's location, contacts, or camera roll

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All receipt details and history you create are stored locally on your device only, using on-device storage. This data is never uploaded, synced, or transmitted to us or to any third party. If you uninstall the app or clear its local data, this information is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered by us, since we never had a copy of it.

3. Permissions the App May Request

  • Storage/Files access — used only to save or share the PDF receipts you generate (e.g. via WhatsApp, email, or your device's file system). Files are only written when you tap "Download" or "Share".

Receipt Maker does not request contacts, precise location, camera, or microphone permissions. The app uses your device's locale/region setting (not GPS) to suggest a default country and currency — this does not require a location permission. The app does request internet access, solely to display AdMob advertisements (see Section 5).

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data, because we do not collect or have access to any data in the first place. If you choose to share a generated PDF receipt via a third-party app (such as WhatsApp or Email), that transfer is governed by that third-party app's own privacy policy, not ours.

5. Advertising

Receipt Maker displays banner and rewarded video advertisements through Google AdMob. To serve these ads, Google AdMob may collect and process device-level information such as your advertising ID, general device information, and approximate location, in accordance with Google's own privacy practices. This data collection is performed by Google, not by us, and is separate from the receipt data described above (which never leaves your device). You can review Google's practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy. Watching a rewarded ad is entirely optional and is only used to unlock additional templates or additional daily receipt generations.

6. Children's Privacy

Receipt Maker is a general-purpose business/personal utility tool and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect any information from children, as we do not collect information from any user.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app's functionality. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Effective date."
